## üìã Quick Replace Guide

Use find and replace in your README.md file:

1. `[Your Name]` ‚Üí Your actual name
2. `Your Location üåç` ‚Üí Your city/country
3. `your.email@gmail.com` ‚Üí Your email
4. `https://linkedin.com/in/yourprofile` ‚Üí Your LinkedIn URL
5. `https://twitter.com/your-handle` ‚Üí Your Twitter URL
6. `https://yourportfolio.com` ‚Üí Your portfolio URL
7. `project1`, `project2`, etc. ‚Üí Your actual repository names
8. Update the `developer` object with your real information
